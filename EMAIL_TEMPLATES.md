## Notes for You:

1. **Set up email forwarding:** Configure support@cartuplift.com to forward to your personal email in your domain settings

2. **Create canned responses:** Save these templates in your email client (Gmail, Outlook) as canned responses for faster replies

3. **Track metrics:** Use a simple spreadsheet or tool like Front/Help Scout to track:
   - Response times
   - Common questions
   - Customer satisfaction

4. **Automate where possible:** Set up auto-replies for nights/weekends pointing to help docs

5. **Be personal:** Always use the merchant's name and reference their specific store/situation when possible
